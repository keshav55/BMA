About Us

For many years, California has ranked 49 among the 50 states in its education system. Public schools consistently fall behind when it comes to Math, Science, Reading, and Grammar. With Brilliant Minds Academy, you can ensure that elementary and middle school students accelerate academically with tutoring in private school level math and reading comprehension. High school students can receive the tutoring and guidance needed to get into the top colleges. Our team includes people with immense teaching experience, as well as students who have recently graduated from Ivy League universities.
